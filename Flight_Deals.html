Booking Methods

Online booking

Through our easy-to-use online booking service, offered via aegeanair.com and the Aegean app, you can simply and quickly book your flight for all GREEK AIRLINES destinations as well as code-share destinations.
You just have to:

  • enter your departure city/airport and arrival city/airport
  • select one-way flights, return flights or multiple destinations
  • choose desired travel dates
  • set the number of passengers by type (adults, children or infants) and
  • apply a promotional code if available

You will then be presented with all flights offered according to your selected route and dates.
You can also use the Low Fare Calendar so as to find the lowest prices available for your trip! Choose a destination and search for the dates with the lowest prices up to 11 months ahead.

Extra options

While booking your flight, you have the opportunity to:

  • choose from various fare types offering different baggage allowance, conditions to change or cancel your flight, discounts for children and infants, priority in check-in or baggage handling, lounge access, seat selection and mileage accrual.
  • request special assistance, select your seat, arrange extra baggage at a discount, add security fast track, insure your trip and order a special meal.

Other booking methods

Apart from booking your flights through GREEK AIRLINES’s digital channels, you may also:

  • ontact our Call Center and one of our Agents will be pleased to assist you; please note that booking charges may be higher than the online booking fee.
  • address your request at any GREEK AIRLINES contracted Airport Ticketing Office or
  • make your booking through your local Travel Agent

My booking

Upon making your flight booking, you have the chance to manage your reservation online whenever you wish to. You just have to enter My booking (using your booking reference and last name), where you will find all your active bookings and will be able to:

  • change your reservation (depending on your fare type)
  • edit your contact details
  • add (extra) baggage or excess baggage weight
  • select your seat
  • add security fast track
  • insure your travel
  • select a special meal
  • access online check-in from 48 hours up to 30 minutes before your flight

    Alternative methods of payment

    PayPal

    PayPal is an online payment service by which you can make secure and convenient online transactions. To use this method of payment, you must register with your personal and account details.

    EPS (offered in Austria)

    EPS is the most popular bank transfer scheme in Austria. It offers shoppers a simple and secure option for making transactions over the Internet. Shoppers log in using their existing credentials to make payments directly to the merchant, which means that payments are made from within the security of their banking environment.

    Bancontact (offered in Belgium)

    Bancontact is the Belgian market leader in electronic payment services. Bancontact determines how money is transferred from the payer's account to the recipient's account.

    iDEAL (offered in Netherlands)

    iDEAL is a payment method made up of a collection of agreements and standards for an immediate online transfer from a purchaser’s bank account to the bank account of a webshop or organization.

    Entercash (offered in Finland)

    Entercash provides a secure, local, real-time payment solution allowing customers to pay directly from their own bank account. This method is available for customers who own a bank account in Finland.

    Alipay (offered in China)

    Alipay is a popular payment method that shoppers based in China use to make payments online from their Alipay eWallet

    UnionPay (offered in China)

    UnionPay is a convenient payment method which ensures the safety of cross-border online payment, through multiple security technology.

    Klarna online bank transfer (offered in Germany, Belgium, Austria, Switzerland, Italy, Spain)

    Klarna is an online direct payment method and works on the basis of online banking. There is no need to register or open a virtual account, known as a wallet. It is an immediate and direct transfer of funds.

    Changes & Cancellations

    Do you want to change or cancel your booking?

    Sometimes travel plans change and you may need to amend your booking. With GREEK AIRLINES, you can do it at any time!

    1. The conditions applying for booking changes and cancellations differ depending on the fare class and fare category you have chosen to travel. Additionally, the change and cancellation rules apply per route.

    • Specific fares may involve charges for making changes (date, flight or route).
    • Any change of name is allowed only in particular cases with a fee.
    • Ticket change or reissue should be completed at the same time.
    • For refundable tickets, there is a refund charge for both domestic and international flights.
    • In case of not showing up for a flight and regarding specific fare categories, there may be a charge or changes (in date, flight or route) may not be allowed.
    • Please check all fare conditions in detail below.

    2. As far as redemption tickets (Miles+Bonus Program/Alpha Bonus Scheme/€pistrofi loyalty Program/Bank of Cyprus antamivi Scheme) are concerned, the following apply:

    • You can change your ticket's date and time, up to 30 min before the flight's departure time, upon a re-issue fee of 20 EUR per ticket.
    • Changes are permitted on the same fare category.
    • Rerouting is not permitted
    • Tax difference is collected, if any.

    3. The process of changing a reservation can be completed through My Booking via all GREEK AIRLINES digital channels. However, the following transactions can be made only through our Call Center*:

    • Changes on partially used tickets.
    • For return tickets or tickets with multiple segments, in case of not showing up at the first flight’s departure, any changes to the subsequent flights.
    • Reroutings (changing the points of departure and/or destination).
    • Individual modifications in a party reservation.
    • Booking cancellations.

    For codeshare flights, the fare rules and terms of transfer of the Airline operating the corresponding flight apply.

    Has your flight been cancelled? Find your options.

    • Proceed with a request for Credit Voucher of equal value to the ticket purchased. For cancelled flights from 01/07/2020 and onwards, which are related to the mandatory adaptation of our future flight scheduling based on the new circumstances, we offer an additional 10% on the Credit Voucher value. You will receive it as soon as possible and you will be able to redeem your Credit Voucher on any future flight within the next 18 months. If you will not use it, you are eligible to request a monetary refund, either for the total or the remaining unused amount.
    • Proceed with rebooking your flight at no cost only through our Call Center.
    • In case you do not want to proceed with a Credit Voucher or a rebooking, you may request for refund for your future cancelled flight

    Credit Voucher info

    Credit Voucher for flights cancelled by GREEK AIRLINES

    To assist our passengers with cancelled flights to better adjust their travel plans, especially those who do not want to rebook for a future date at this time, we offer a Credit Voucher of equal value to the ticket purchased (including all segments of the booking).

    Credit Voucher for bookings voluntarily cancelled by passengers

    In case you have a flight, which has not been cancelled by GREEK AIRLINES, but you wish to voluntarily cancel your ticket, you may submit a request to receive a Credit Voucher, through GREEK AIRLINES Call Center or at specific airport stations in Greece.
