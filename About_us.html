Profile

We are a member of Star Alliance and Greece’s largest Airline, providing at our inception in 1999 until today, full service, premium quality, short and medium haul services.

On the 23rd of October 2013, we acquired Olympic Air; as a result, passengers are now provided with increased flight frequencies and connections as well as improved accessibility to Greece’s islands, including some of the more remote. We invest in strengthening our international presence and supporting the Greek tourism both in Athens and regional airports.

As an outcome of our efforts, in 2019 we transferred almost 15 million passengers.GREEK AIRLINES is consistently one of the most successful airlines in Europe and is constantly voted by the passengers among the best in terms of service and comfort.



GREEK AIRLINES has been announced as the Best Regional Airline in Europe for 10th consecutive year and 11th time in the last 12 years. Moreover, GREEK AIRLINES was named 3rd Best Regional Airline in the Word, was ranked 10th in Europe for its cabin crew, was amongst the World's Top 100 Airlines for 2021 (36th) and was amongst the winners of the COVID-19 Airline Excellence Award 2021 category, that rate the COVID-19 standards and best practices for the health and safety measures applied for both passengers and crew on board and on the ground.

In line with this, our 2022 timetable includes a network of 160 destinations, 31 domestic and 129 international in 44 countries. The flights are performed with one of the youngest fleets in Europe, comprising 65 aircraft, while by the end of 2022 the company will have 68 aircraft.

Cargo

Cargo

If you are looking for specialized transportation of your merchandise, you can trust our 14 years of experience!

Our Cargo department was set up in 2001 in order to offer full services to our customers. Today, our cargo department operates almost across the whole of the company’s network to 145 internationals as well as Greek destinations, offering airport-to-airport services for freight carriers, import/export companies, postal and courier firms and private individuals needing to transport items by freight.

All the department’s operations are focused on immediate service for our customers. The exceptional on-time performance levels of the company’s network, and the way freight bookings are handled, both allow us to provide an immediate response to more than 95% of customer’s freight requests (Fly as Booked) on a daily basis.

The booking system that operates from March 2006 offers to our customers the opportunity to get informed via live tracking for the progress of the delivery of their merchandise, from the moment of the booking until the final delivery to the recipient.

  • 14 years of experience
  • Exceptional on-time performance levels
  • Sophisticated management system for bookings and transportation
  • 24/7, 365 days per year

Operating Hours

Athens Cargo department operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, processing phone bookings and taking delivery of cargo consignments for almost all destinations of the company’s network.

Operating 24 hours daily, we are able to move all consignments using the very next flight to the final destination, avoiding delays and keeping safe all vulnerable and perishable products.

Charter

Along with our scheduled flights, in GREEK AIRLINES we also offer charter flights all year long with the fleet of aircraft of GREEK AIRLINES with capacity from 37 up to 220 seats, to Travel Agencies, Tour Operators and prestigious Companies - for their business travel - within Europe and Greece.


AIRBUS 320-200 with 220 seats which perform a series of charter flights mainly on weekly frequency from/to Italy, Sweden, Denmark, Finland, Norway, Hungary, Slovenia, Israel, Romania, Serbia, Russia, Armenia and Moldova, in cooperation with major Tour Operators of relevant countries. Ad-hoc flights are operated upon aircraft availability.

There is a steady course of charter operations every year which is a result of the efficiency and reliability that GREEK AIRLINES offers to its clients. For all of us in GREEK AIRLINES, it is really remarkable that the dynamic growth along with the rendering of high quality services to our passengers, led to the stable growth of charter flights which played an important role to the expansion of our network to new destinations. GREEK AIRLINES Charter Operations can be easily considered as a profitable bridge between all Europe and Greece.
